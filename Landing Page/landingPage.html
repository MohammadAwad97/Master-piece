Who we are and what we do!

ClicFlyer provides Business Intelligence Analytics and Tools to retailers and brands to elevate their business plans using data science. Leaflet promotions roughly drive an average of 55% of modern trade sales in the region and with the exponential growth in data an effective BI tool is essential to extract key insights that will fuel critical decisions.. Our SaaS Cloud based Business Intelligence (BI) Tools help retailers as well as brands to optimize their promotional strategy. We have over 8 years of historical, first person data that enables retailers and brands to keep a tab on their promotion activities and track the data of other players and competitors in the market and use this information to formulate the most favorable promotion plans through offers and flyers. Navigating real time data combined with massive historical data provides insights into promotional strategy and depths of consumer behaviors and ultimately leads to increasing sales and profits. Our BI Dashboard and tool is the ultimate game changer in the world of retail analytics. Our Retail Analytics Services are used by many leading Retailers and Brands. We cover a wide range of categories including Electronics, Grocery, Baby & Mom, Healthy and Beauty, Home, Furniture & Pets, Bakery, Fruits and Vegetables, Frozen Foods, Meat and Fish, Laundry and Cleaning and Paper and Disposables. We are committed to providing timely actionable insights to improve your Return on your Promotion investment. We currently operate in the United Arab Emirates, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Kuwait, Egypt, Qatar, Oman, Jordan and South Africa.
